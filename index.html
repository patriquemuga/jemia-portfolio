Our Story

Established in 1991, Jemia Stationers and Printers has been serving businesses and individuals with premium printing and stationery solutions for over three decades. What started as a small local shop has grown into a comprehensive printing service with state-of-the-art equipment and experienced professionals.

Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the most reliable printing services in the region. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional results for every project.

With 34 years of industry experience, we've helped thousands of clients bring their ideas to life through print. Whether you need corporate stationery, marketing materials, or custom printing solutions, Jemia is your trusted partner.
